MyLab & Mastering is the world's leading collection of online homework, tutorial, and assessment products designed with a single purpose in mind: to improve the results of all higher education students, one student at a time.

With input from more than 11 million student users annually, MyLab & Mastering creates online learning experiences that are truly personalized and continuously adaptive. MyLab & Mastering reacts to how students are actually per-forming, offering data-driven guidance that helps them better absorb course material and understand difficult concepts.
